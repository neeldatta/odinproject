Pizza

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Yeast
  • Salt
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, water, yeast, and salt together to make dough.
  2. Let dough rise for 1 hour.
  3. Roll out dough and add tomato sauce, cheese, and pepperoni.
  4. Bake at 400 degrees for 15 minutes.
